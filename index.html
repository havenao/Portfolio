Hello World! I'm Haven.

I love learning and creating. While I have many creative interests, such as Art, Graphic Design, Music, Videography, and Linguistics, I have decided to pursue a career as a Programmer.

Computer programming ties my interests together into one medium, and that is the purpose of this site: to explore and combine my passions while sharpening my web development skills

I am a Programmer

I got in to programming by learning to make small games using Unity. I quickly realized that this was what I wanted to do for a career. I signed up for the Academy Pittsburgh developer bootcamp, where I solidified and expanded my programming skills. Check out my projects here.

I am an Artist and Graphic Designer

Prior to beginning my programming journey, I focused on graphic design and visual art. I am very much a visually oriented thinker, so good design is a top priority for me in any project.

I have always loved sketching, and recently picked up some digital art tools. Check out my recent works here.

I am a Musician

I play guitar and write songs for the Pittsburgh-based rock band, Moonspeaker.

Music is my therapy. It's how I cope with the weight of the world. Hopefully you can find something in our music that hits you the right way.
